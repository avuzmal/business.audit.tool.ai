# Phase 3: The MCP-Powered Follow-Up Engine & Launch Playbook

## PART A: The MCP-Powered Follow-Up Engine (Antigravity)
Instead of manually copying data from Google Sheets to draft emails, the Antigravity Agent with native Google Workspace MCP access acts as an autonomous SDR (Sales Development Representative).

### Workflow
1. **Read** the Google Sheet "Audit_Leads". Filter for rows where Column L (Status) is "New".
2. **Extract**: Business Name, First Name (from email), Industry, Biggest Pain Point, and "Opp 1 Title" from the audit.
3. **Draft** a hyper-personalized email in the Gmail "Drafts" folder.
4. **Update** the Google Sheet Status to "Drafted".

### Email Drafting Rules
- **Subject Line**: Lowercase, casual, referencing their business name or specific pain point. (e.g., "quick question about [Business Name]'s workflow")
- **Length**: Maximum 4 sentences. No fluff.
- **Hook**: Mention their exact "Biggest Pain Point".
- **Value**: Reference "Opportunity #1" and state the specific hours saved/ROI.
- **CTA**: Ask for a low-friction 15-minute chat. Provide a Calendly link.
- **Tone**: Peer-to-peer, helpful, zero corporate jargon.

## PART B: The 3-Touch Nurture Sequence
**Touch 1: Day 0**
The MCP Drafted Email (as configured above).

**Touch 2: Day 3 (The Value Add)**
Subject: one thing i forgot to mention
"Hi [First Name],
I was reviewing your audit again and realized I didn’t emphasize one thing: the [Specific Tool] sync we discussed can be built without disrupting your current team’s workflow.
No need to reply if you’re swamped, but if you want to see a 2-minute Loom video of how this works in practice, just reply 'yes' and I’ll send it over.
Best, [Your Name]"

**Touch 3: Day 7 (The Breakup / Permission to Close)**
Subject: should i close your file?
"Hi [First Name],
I don’t want to clutter your inbox. I’ll assume automating the [Pain Point] isn’t a priority for [Business Name] right now, so I’ll close your audit file on my end.
If things change in the future, the audit report I sent will always be in your inbox as a reference.
Wishing you a great Q3.
Best, [Your Name]"

## PART C: Launch & Distribution Playbook
1. **LinkedIn "Build in Public" Post (Day 1)**: Give the tool away to the first 50 commenters.
2. **Niche Community Drop (Day 3)**: Share on Reddit (r/smallbusiness, r/SaaS) and IndieHackers.
3. **Warm Network Reactivation (Day 5)**: Personalized DM/email to 20 past clients/colleagues.
4. **Productized Service Listing (Day 7)**: List on Fiverr/Upwork as a $49 paid gig.

## PART D: The Conversion Protocol (15-Minute Call)
- **The Frame (2 mins)**: Confirm Opportunity #1 priority.
- **The Validation (5 mins)**: Let them vent about the pain point.
- **The Solution (5 mins)**: Pitch the n8n/AI workflow.
- **The Close (3 mins)**: Mention investment ($1,500 - $2,500) and payback period. Send agreement.

## Success Metrics
- **Form Completion Rate**: > 60%
- **PDF Open Rate**: > 70%
- **Reply Rate to Touch 1**: > 25%
- **Booked Call Rate**: > 10%
- **Close Rate**: > 30%
